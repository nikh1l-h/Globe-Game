Globe Game

How to Play

This is a game about countries.

Your job is to guess the mystery country before the time limit.

To help you, you can guess any country on the globe and it will be given a colour representing how close it is to the mystery country.

Good guesses and correct answers will be rewarded with score. The aim is to get the highest score possible without running out of time.

Enjoy!
